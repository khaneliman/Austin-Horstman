About Fox Valley Technical College

Fox Valley Technical College is a leading technical college in Wisconsin, providing comprehensive education in technology and skilled trades. The IT Software Development program offers hands-on training in programming, database management, web development, and software engineering principles, preparing students for careers in the technology industry.

Program Highlights & Skills Acquired

  • • Programming fundamentals and software development lifecycle
  • • Database design, implementation, and management
  • • Web development technologies and frameworks
  • • Object-oriented programming concepts and practices
  • • Software testing, debugging, and quality assurance
  • • Project management and team collaboration skills
