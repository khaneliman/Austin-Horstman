Project Overview

The DoItBest Platform project involved developing a comprehensive e-commerce and inventory management system for one of the largest hardware retailers in North America. This multi-faceted platform serves both B2B and B2C customers, handling complex inventory management, supplier integrations, and multi-location fulfillment.

Working closely with DoItBest's business stakeholders and technical teams, we modernized their entire digital infrastructure while maintaining seamless operations across thousands of retail locations and distribution centers. The solution encompasses customer-facing e-commerce, dealer portals, inventory management, and supply chain optimization.

The platform successfully increased online sales conversion rates, improved inventory accuracy, and streamlined operations for both corporate locations and independent dealers within the DoItBest network.

Key Features & Capabilities

Multi-Channel Commerce

Unified platform supporting B2B dealer portals and B2C e-commerce with integrated inventory and pricing management.

Advanced Inventory System

Real-time inventory tracking across multiple warehouses and retail locations with automated reorder points.

Fulfillment Optimization

Intelligent order routing and fulfillment optimization for faster delivery and reduced shipping costs.

Dealer Network Portal

Dedicated portal for independent dealers with custom pricing, bulk ordering, and business analytics tools.
