Project Overview

ClearTrend was a sophisticated healthcare analytics platform designed to help healthcare organizations gain deep insights into their clinical and operational data. The system processed complex healthcare datasets to generate meaningful reports and visualizations.

Working as part of the Skyline Technologies consulting team, I contributed to the development of key features including data visualization components, reporting engines, and user interface improvements that enhanced the platform's usability and performance.

The platform served healthcare executives, clinical directors, and operational managers by providing them with actionable insights to improve patient care and optimize organizational efficiency.
